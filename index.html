Our story

Started with one churn and a very stubborn opinion about vanilla.

Scoop & Swirl opened as a two-person operation with a single ice cream churn and a firm belief that vanilla should never be an afterthought. Years later, the churn count has gone up — but our commitment to quality hasn't budged.

Every base is cooked in-house each morning, every mix-in is something we'd eat by the spoonful on its own, and every flavor gets tasted by the whole team before it earns a spot on the board.

  • Milk and cream sourced from a farm 20 minutes away
  • New seasonal flavor dropped every two weeks
  • Dairy-free base available in every rotation
